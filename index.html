FAQ

Common questions

From travelers like you

Is this a medical diagnosis?

No. We are licensed nurses, not doctors. We do not diagnose, treat, or prescribe. Medical care — including online consultations and medical certificates — is provided by licensed partner doctors. Our role is to guide, arrange, and support you throughout.

What languages do you support?

English, Spanish, and easy Japanese (やさしい日本語). Spanish is our specialty — our nurses consult directly in Spanish, without machine translation.

What does the ¥30,000 fee include? Are doctor's fees included?

The fee covers the full concierge service: nurse consultation, hospital navigation, online doctor appointment booking and arrangement, document support, and follow-up. Fees charged by the medical institution itself — consultation fees, medicine, tests — are paid separately to the clinic or covered by your travel insurance.

How does the online doctor consultation work?

We book an appointment with a partner clinic that offers telemedicine. You talk to a licensed doctor by video call, usually the same day. Depending on the doctor's judgment, medicine can be prescribed and arranged. We prepare a symptom summary in Japanese beforehand so the consultation goes smoothly.

Can I get a medical certificate for my travel insurance?

Yes — medical certificates are issued by the examining doctor, and we handle the process for you: requesting it, following up with the clinic, receiving it, and explaining its contents in your language so you can submit it to your insurer.

What are your hours? What about the middle of the night?

We answer from 8:00 to 22:00 JST every day. Outside these hours, for anything urgent, call 119 (ambulance) or visit an emergency hospital. The Japan Visitor Hotline (050-3816-2787) also operates 24/7.

How do I pay?

Credit card or PayPal. We send you a secure payment link by LINE, WhatsApp, or email.

Important notice
  • This service is provided by licensed nurses and is not a medical institution. We do not perform diagnosis, treatment, or prescription of medicine.
